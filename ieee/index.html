Why Link Camp?

LINK CAMP 2025 is the premier selection ground for passionate IEEE members aspiring to take up student leadership positions in various IEEE societies and the prestigious LINK Team.

It is not just an event; it is a platform to prove your mettle. With over 700+ participants, Link Camp offers an unparalleled networking opportunity, allowing you to connect with the brightest minds across the section while undergoing a rigorous selection process.
